# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We aim to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone participating in SwipeHire.

## Expected Behavior
Participants should:
- Be respectful and constructive
- Use inclusive language
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Support others in learning and growing

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples include:
- Harassment or bullying
- Discrimination of any kind
- Posting offensive or violent content
- Personal attacks or insults

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or see unacceptable behavior:
- Contact the project maintainers privately

All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may:
- Warn the offender
- Remove contributions
- Ban individuals from the project

---

Thank you for helping keep SwipeHire respectful and supportive!
